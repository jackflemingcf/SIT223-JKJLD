Support for International Students

Connecting international students who require assistance with organisations who can support them during a time of need.

Examples of charities we work with

Food Bank

Foodbank is the largest hunger relief charity in Australia. We are the pantry to the charity sector, linking surplus food and groceries to people in need.

OZ Harvest

OzHarvest is Australia's leading food rescue organisation. At our core, we save surplus food & deliver to charities that help people in need.
